About Us

CalMates Interpreting and Translation Services strives to assist private and public agencies with excellent language services in the Los Angeles County of California, making the County a more understanding place to live and work. We make a difference in the lives of diverse communities, by helping them avoid any language barriers that would impede them from getting fully integrated in the American society.
Ms. Anahit Maryanyan is the business leader and founder. Ms. Maryanyan has extensive simultaneous interpreting expertise of over 25 years, abroad and locally. The professional team of linguists under her leadership ensures that the language assignments meet the high quality standards and meet the terms requested by our Clients.
